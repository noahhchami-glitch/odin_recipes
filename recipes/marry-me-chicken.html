Marry Me Chicken

Description

A luscious, creamy homemade Marry Me Chicken that’s packed with flavor and sure to impress.

Juicy chicken breasts are seared to perfection and smothered in a rich sauce of cream, sun-dried tomatoes, garlic, and Parmesan.

Perfect for a romantic dinner or a comforting weeknight meal that feels special.

Ingredients

  • 2–3 boneless, skinless chicken breasts
  • Salt and freshly ground black pepper

  • 1–2 tbsp olive oil
  • 2–3 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1/2 cup sun-dried tomatoes, chopped
  • 1 cup heavy cream
  • 1/2 cup chicken broth
  • 1/2 cup freshly grated Parmesan cheese
  • 1 tsp dried Italian herbs (or a mix of basil, oregano, thyme)
  • Fresh parsley, chopped, for garnish

Steps

  1. Prepare the chicken: Season the chicken breasts generously with salt and freshly ground black pepper on both sides.

  2. Sear the chicken: Heat olive oil in a large skillet over medium-high heat.

    Add the chicken and cook 5–7 minutes per side, until golden brown and nearly cooked through.

    Remove the chicken and set aside.

  3. Cook the aromatics: In the same skillet, add the minced garlic and chopped sun-dried tomatoes.

    Sauté for 1–2 minutes until fragrant.

  4. Make the sauce: Pour in the heavy cream and chicken broth.

    Stir in the Parmesan cheese and dried Italian herbs.

    Let the sauce simmer for 3–5 minutes until slightly thickened.

  5. Combine and finish cooking: Return the chicken to the skillet, spooning some sauce over the top.

    Simmer for another 5 minutes, until the chicken is fully cooked and the sauce is creamy.

  6. Serve: Garnish with chopped fresh parsley and serve hot, ideally with pasta, rice, or crusty bread to soak up the sauce.
